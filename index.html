Intro

Full stack software developer with a history of app production, professional improvisation, and world-class hospitality. Eager to find an organization where my imagination and passion for building new ideas can be put to good use. My technical skills paired with my desire to delight and entertain make me a valuable asset to any team. I thrive in new environments and love to find unique ways to bring together artfulness and cutting edge technology.

Born and raised in Bar Harbor, ME, I grew up fascinated with technology. In the 6th grade, I was lucky enough to participate in the Maine Learning Technology Initiative (MLTI) where I was given my first ever laptop: a Mac iBook. I was able to bring it home, and quickly became fascinated at the possibilites that it presented. I kept a keen interest in technology throughout my high school career, using digital video and editing technology for my senior project.

During my college years, I had the luxury of visiting my brother, a music student at Princeton University. There I encountered a budding scene of developers and musicians called the pLork, or Princeton Laptop Orchestra. One of the musicians used an 8 x 8 grid of pushable, light-up buttons called a monome. On another visit, I experienced a performance from a touring musician named Alfred "Daedelus" Darling. In addition to my 8 x 8 monome, Daedelus had a 16 x 16. The performance also changed mys life. After, I began assembling my own monome for an independent study at Hampshire College, and by my senior year I had developed three applications that used the 8 x 8 monome in conjunction with a brand new piece of technology, the arc.

Projects

Coding Projects

Naniel app (Full Stack Web App, Rails Backend / VueJS Frontend): I built a web app from scratch that allows users to donate their art (or other objects of value) directly to charitable causes, allowing the user (artist/collector) to choose what their cause is (or who their Naniel is) and allows them to set either a fixed price or an auction for the item (with 100% of the proceeds going to the people in need). The app also functions as a philanthropic art community where artists can connect and share their concerns for the world. Click here to check out the Naniel App.

In 2017, my collaborative web app Migos Ad Libs gained international acclaim. The website included a database of ad-libs that we used Javascript to time-sync with SoundCloud's music API. A month later my satire/parody rap project XXXSENSATION got a profile from Mass Appeal, and the project has been streamed millions of times and has raised thousands of dollars for Women's Rights and domestic violence prevention organizations.

About

Before COVID-19 I was a regular at The Annoyance Theatre where I performed, wrote, and directed avant-garde comedy shows. Since the pandemic struck, I have focused on education and recently completed the Actualize Boot Camp, as well as several MasterClasses (David Lynch, Tom Morello). I am excited to keep learning and better understanding this strange orb we happen to inhibit.
